I'm Jason Heppler, a 20-something historian, technologist, husband, coder, and web-nerd. I'm the project manager of the William F. Cody Digital Archive at the Center for Digital Research in the Humanities and a doctoral student in history at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln focusing on the twentieth century North American West. I'm also interested in the history of computers, humanities computing, machine intelligence, and digital history. The views expressed here are not those of any current or former employer.
